About Me

I got my start in development over five years ago, learning Lua and building a solid foundation in scripting and problem-solving. For the past three years, I’ve focused on web development, where I enjoy creating clean, intuitive interfaces that put the user first. I care about blending good design with reliable functionality, and I’ve recently been diving into back-end technologies to grow into a more versatile developer.

Outside of coding, I’m passionate about men’s mental health and the value of open, supportive communities. That commitment also shows up in my professional work—I’m part of the Trust and Safety team at rscripts.net, where I help maintain a safe and positive space for everyone.
